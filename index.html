Elevating the City's Green Experience

Redefining the Quezon City landscape, a new Central Business and Lifestyle District emerges. An inspiring City Center. A stunning community. This is Vertis North. Embracing lush greens and contemporary architecture, Alveo Land's first residential community rises within, unfolding a refreshing new vertical living experience. With a fusion of environments, High Park brings forth nature at the core of a dynamic city.

An Unsurpassed Legacy

We rise to the occasion. For more than 80 years, Ayala Land has set the standard for real estate development in the Philippines. Presenting the nation's most highly regarded addresses rangin from an ensemble of sustainably integrated growth centers to multi-generational communities. All vibrant destinations spotlighting a singular vision of enhancing land and enriching lives for more people.

Quezon City, the Emerging Enterprise Capital of the North

Once the nation's capital, today an emerging Asian Hub thriving with over 3 million people. At least 60,000 businesses and more than 700,000 households call Metro Manila's largest city home. A 16,000-hectare cultural, lifestyle, and commercial hotspot, Quezon City is consistently among the highest earning and most progressive cities in the country.

Through the years, Ayala Land has aligned Quezon City's past and present with the future—propelling vast resources and a fiercely entrepreneurial spirit toward building the city's newest lifestyle and enterprise capital.

A Tradition of Excellence in Quezon City

Constantly evolving what we know of urban spaces, Ayala Land continues to lay the groundwork for commerce and progress in the Philippines. Makati Central Business District. Bonifacio Global City. NUVALI. Cebu Park District. This is the heritage we are strengthening in Quezon City.

Amplifying the success of the most highly regarded residences in the North such as Ayala Heights, Ayala Hillside Estates, and Alveo Land's Ferndale Villas. The most dynamic leisure and business scenes, including TriNoma and UP-Ayala Land TechnoHub. In Quezon City, an emerging urban core unfolds, heightening a portfolio of flourishing growth centers throughout the country.

Ingenuity Finds a New Home

Taking Quezon City to new heights, Vertis North rises as the inspiring Central Business District. The North Triangle property on which this destination stands spans 46 hectares of prime land. A location of unparalleled strategic importance bound by EDSA, Quezon Avenue, Agham Road, North Avenue, and Mindanao Avenue. Masterplanned by Ayala Land, Vertis North is the nucleus of a uniquely entrepreneurial city.

An enterprising city. A dynamic engine for growth. At Vertis North, all roads lead to progress.
